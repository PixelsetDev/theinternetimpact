The Internet Impact's Hall of Fame.

We want to recognise the people who've made massive impacts to the internet and the world, so we made this list. It contains those who created core technologies, those who made the internet what it is today, we'll be adding more people to this list over time. If you'd like to suggest an addition, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
